# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist for College Students

1. Think before you post — once online, content is nearly impossible 
   to fully delete
2. Set all social media accounts to private and review follower 
   requests carefully
3. Never share passwords with anyone, including close friends
4. Block and report anyone who sends threatening or hurtful messages
5. Screenshot and save evidence of cyberbullying before blocking
6. Do not retaliate — responding often escalates the situation
7. Talk to a trusted adult, counsellor, or friend if you are 
   being targeted
8. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone online
9. Use only official and verified UPI apps (BHIM, GPay, PhonePe) 
   — avoid clicking payment links sent via SMS or WhatsApp
10. Report cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in or call Helpline 1930 (24x7)
