# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ Do's
1. Keep your profiles professional and up to date
2. Think before you post — ask "would I show this to my professor?"
3. Use privacy settings to control who sees your content
4. Engage respectfully in online discussions and communities
5. Share academic achievements, projects, and learning milestones

## ❌ Don'ts
1. Don't share personal information like phone number or address publicly
2. Don't post or forward unverified news or rumours
3. Don't engage with or respond to cyberbullies — report instead
4. Don't use social media to express frustration about college/faculty
5. Don't accept friend/follow requests from unknown accounts
